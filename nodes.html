List of all Nodes¶

pySPACE comes along with a big choice of processing nodes. They can be accessed via NodeChainOperation. In the following you can get an overview on their functionality, the mapping from node names in specification files to the node class and vice versa.

For details on the usage of the nodes and for getting usage examples, have a look at their documentation.
